Newcomb's Problem

The Subject

You are presented with a choice between two boxes

Omega

A superintelligent being who can predict your choices

Box 1

$1,000

Box 2

$1,000,000 or $0

The Dilemma:

In Newcomb's problem, you have two choices:

  1. Take both boxes: Get the $1,000 plus whatever is in Box 2
  2. Take only the opaque box: Get whatever is in Box 2

The twist is that Omega has already predicted your choice and filled the boxes accordingly:

  • If Omega predicted you would take only Box 2, it put $1,000,000 in it.
  • If Omega predicted you would take both boxes, it put nothing ($0) in Box 2.

What would you choose?
